COPPER

A BIT ABOUT COPPER

Copper is a wide used metal, it is used in thousands of objects and processes. It’s used because of how easy it is to form. It is also used because of its high thermal and electrical conductivity. Copper is the oldest metal to be used by humans. over 10,000 thousand years ago humans were using it. It was also the first metal to be melted down in a mold to make something like a pot or a sword.

HOW DO YOU SAY IT?

Cop-er

WHERE IS IT USED?

Below are some objects that copper is used in.

WIRES

Most wires are made of Copper because it has such a high conductivity rate.

PIPING

Most plumbing pipes are copper due to the high heat conductivity so they keep things warmer for longer.

COINS

A long time ago we would form copper into coins and used it as currency, we also use it today in 1p’s and 2p’s.

INSTRUMENTS

Most Trumpets, Saxophones and even organs are made with a copper alloy called bronze.

GENERATORS

Spools of Copper wire are used with special magnets to generate electricity.
