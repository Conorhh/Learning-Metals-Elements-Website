A BIT ABOUT SILVER

Silver is a precious metal that is shiny and metallic in its natural form. It is the best conductor of heat and electricity out of all the metals on the periodic table. Most silver that is mined now days will be turned into Sterling Silver which is a combination of 92% silver and 8% copper, this is to strengthen the silver. Like copper, silver was discovered a long time ago dating back thousands of years, the latest beingg 3000 BC from the Sumer civilization

WHERE IS IT USED?

SILVERWEAR

Due to silvers high value, some people make silver into cutlery for special occasions.

FILLINGS

Most fillings are silver amalgam which is silver mixed with zinc and other metals. This is to stop erosion.

JEWELLERY

Silver has been used for jewellery since ancient times because of it rarity and shiny colour.
