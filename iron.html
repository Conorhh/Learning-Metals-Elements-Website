A BIT ABOUT IRON

Iron is the most abundant metal on earth due to it making up most of the earths outer crust and inner core. Like copper it has a high conductivity rate with heat and electricity. It is also very easily formed because it is so soft. Iron is also the most naturally magnetic metal on the periodic table. Iron is the number 1 used metal to create alloys. It is mixed together with Carbon and other elements to create Iron alloys such as steel, cast iron and pig iron..

WHERE IS IT USED?

Below are some things that iron is used in.

A lot of pots and pans now days are made of cast iron due to its resillience and natural non-stick properties

GOLF CLUBS

Most golf club heads are made of Steel, this is because it’s light and strong enough to hit the ball far enough.

CLOTHES IRON

Surprisingly a clothes iron is actually made of iron. Iron is used because of it’s high heat conductivity.

BLOOD

Iron is a key part of the blood in our bodies because it is the main part that carries the oxygen around.

BOATS
